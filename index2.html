About Us

Established in 1999, Siliguri Institute of Technology (SIT) is the largest private educational institution running under the vision of Techno India Group and is only one of its kinds in North Bengal, West Bengal, India. About 5 km away from Siliguri city, the Institute has a sprawling and picturesque campus surrounded by opulent lush green tea gardens, dense forests of Sukna with a backdrop of Sub-Himalayan ranges making it a premier abode of learning. The institute runs B.Tech Programs (CSE, IT, ECE, ECE, CE), Undergraduate and Postgraduate Courses in Management (MBA, BBA) & Computer Applications (MCA, BCA), Undergraduate Programs in Hotel Management & Catering Technology (HMCT), Hospitality Management, Travel & Tourism Managment, Hospital Managment, along with Diploma Programs (CST, ECT, EE, CE, ME) in involving more than 4000 student

VISION & MISSION

  • VISION OF INSTITUTE: To be a recognised institution offering high quality education, opportunities to students to become globally employable Engineers/Professionals in best ranked industries and research organisation.
  • MISSION OF INSTITUTE: To impart quality technical education for holistic development of students who will fulfil the needs of the industry/society and be actively engaged in making a successful career in industry/research/higher education in India & abroad.
